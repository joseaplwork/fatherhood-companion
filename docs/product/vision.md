# Product Vision

## What

Grove Companion is an AI-first emotional support platform for co-parents navigating separation
and shared custody.

## Why

Co-parents navigating separation face a unique combination of challenges: custody logistics, child
development decisions, emotional isolation, and the complexity of co-ordinating with another
parent — often without adequate support systems. This platform exists to close that gap through
persistent AI support, peer community, and practical tools tailored to their journey.

## Who

Any parent sharing custody of their children: recently separated, long-term co-parents, and those
managing the emotional weight of divided households. The platform meets them where they are and
grows with them.

## Core Value Proposition

- Never feel alone: an AI companion is always available, and a community of co-parents is nearby.
- Stay organized: one calendar, all dimensions of co-parenting in one place.
- Keep growing: curated, AI-enhanced resources matched to where you are in your journey.

---

## The Five Pillars

### 1. Dashboard

A personal insights hub. Reflects the co-parent's emotional state over time, surfaces mood
patterns, highlights upcoming events, and surfaces AI-generated observations about their journey.
The entry point into the platform each session.

### 2. AI Chat Support

A private, conversational AI companion designed to provide emotional support, practical advice,
and guided reflection. Always available, non-judgmental, and context-aware — it remembers the
co-parent's story across sessions.

### 3. Community

A space where co-parents can connect, share, and support each other. Includes threaded
discussions, reactions, and organized real-world or virtual events: meetups, support groups, and
workshops. Community is human-to-human; AI assists with discovery and connection but does not
impersonate members.

### 4. Calendar

A unified calendar that manages personal events, child-related appointments, co-parenting
schedules, community events, and custody arrangements. Children can be registered by their
parents, and their schedules are reflected alongside the co-parent's in a single view.

### 5. Resources

A curated, AI-enhanced library of practical content: mental health, co-parenting strategy, legal
guidance, child development, finance, and self-care. Content is contextualized to the user's
situation and journey stage. AI surfaces what is most relevant, not just what is most recent.

---

## AI as a First-Class Feature

Every pillar has an AI layer. AI is not a chatbot bolted on — it is the connective tissue of the
platform.

| Pillar | AI Role |
|---|---|
| Dashboard | Pattern recognition, mood trend analysis, personalized insights |
| Chat | Persistent emotional companion with long-term context |
| Community | Connection suggestions, event relevance ranking |
| Calendar | Conflict detection, self-care nudges, schedule summaries |
| Resources | Semantic search, personalized recommendations, AI-enhanced content |

---

## Non-Goals (for now)

- This is not a legal advice platform. Legal content is educational only.
- This is not a shared platform for both co-parents simultaneously. Each user has their own
  private account and perspective; co-parent data is stored privately per user.
- This is not a children's platform. Children are represented as data, not users.
