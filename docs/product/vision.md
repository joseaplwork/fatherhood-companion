# Product Vision

## What

Fatherhood Companion is an AI-first emotional support platform for single fathers navigating
parenthood alone.

## Why

Single fathers face a unique combination of challenges: custody logistics, child development
decisions, emotional isolation, and the weight of being the sole caregiver — often without
adequate support systems. This platform exists to close that gap through persistent AI support,
peer community, and practical tools tailored to their journey.

## Who

Single fathers at any stage: recently separated, long-term solo parents, and those co-parenting
while carrying the emotional weight alone. The platform meets them where they are and grows with
them.

## Core Value Proposition

- Never feel alone: an AI companion is always available, and a community of fathers is nearby.
- Stay organized: one calendar, all dimensions of fatherhood in one place.
- Keep growing: curated, AI-enhanced resources matched to where you are in your journey.

---

## The Five Pillars

### 1. Dashboard

A personal insights hub. Reflects the father's emotional state over time, surfaces mood patterns,
highlights upcoming events, and surfaces AI-generated observations about his journey. The entry
point into the platform each session.

### 2. AI Chat Support

A private, conversational AI companion designed to provide emotional support, practical advice,
and guided reflection. Always available, non-judgmental, and context-aware — it remembers the
father's story across sessions.

### 3. Community

A space where fathers can connect, share, and support each other. Includes threaded discussions,
reactions, and organized real-world or virtual events: meetups, support groups, and workshops.
Community is human-to-human; AI assists with discovery and connection but does not impersonate
members.

### 4. Calendar

A unified calendar that manages personal events, child-related appointments, co-parenting
schedules, community events, and custody arrangements. Children can be registered by their parents,
and their schedules are reflected alongside the father's in a single view.

### 5. Resources

A curated, AI-enhanced library of practical content: mental health, co-parenting strategy, legal
guidance, child development, finance, and self-care. Content is contextualized to the user's
situation and journey stage. AI surfaces what is most relevant, not just what is most recent.

---

## AI as a First-Class Feature

Every pillar has an AI layer. AI is not a chatbot bolted on — it is the connective tissue of the
platform.

| Pillar | AI Role |
|---|---|
| Dashboard | Pattern recognition, mood trend analysis, personalized insights |
| Chat | Persistent emotional companion with long-term context |
| Community | Connection suggestions, event relevance ranking |
| Calendar | Conflict detection, self-care nudges, schedule summaries |
| Resources | Semantic search, personalized recommendations, AI-enhanced content |

---

## Non-Goals (for now)

- This is not a legal advice platform. Legal content is educational only.
- This is not a co-parenting coordination tool for both parents. It serves the single father's
  perspective exclusively.
- This is not a children's platform. Children are represented as data, not users.
